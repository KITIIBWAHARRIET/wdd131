Uganda: Population, Capital, Languages, and Weather

Country Data

Population

Uganda is a vibrant country with an estimated population of 46 million people. Urban areas like Kampala, Entebbe, and Jinja are growing rapidly, while rural communities maintain traditional farming and village life.

Capital

The capital city of Uganda is Kampala, known for its bustling markets, historic landmarks such as the Kasubi Tombs and Uganda Museum, and as the political and economic hub of the nation.

Language

Uganda is multilingual. English is the official language, Swahili is recognized as a second national language, and there are over 40 indigenous languages, including Luganda, Runyankole, Luo, and Ateso.
