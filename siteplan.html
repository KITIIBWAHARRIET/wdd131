HealthyBites

HealthyBites promotes healthy eating and lifestyle tips for busy professionals.

Site Purpose

The website provides articles, recipes, and meal planning tools to help users maintain a healthy diet. It also includes a newsletter signup for weekly tips.

Scenarios

  • What are quick healthy recipes I can make in under 30 minutes?
  • How do I plan my weekly meals efficiently for better nutrition?

Color Schema

  • #2a7f62 - Dark Green: used for headings and buttons.
  • #ff6f61 - Coral: used for accents, links, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

  • Arial: body text and paragraphs.
  • Verdana: headings (h1, h2).
